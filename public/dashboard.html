Intent Guide

HOT  Actively looking, ready to take action now.
e.g. "need a realtor", "house hunting", "ready to buy"

WARM  In research/consideration mode.
e.g. "thinking about moving", "renting vs buying", "home prices"

COLD  General interest, not urgent.
e.g. "iowa housing market", "mortgage rates", "home values"

Client Settings Explained

Lead Limit
Max number of new (uncontacted) leads the engine will hold for this client at once. Once the limit is hit, ingestion pauses until you mark leads as contacted or sold.

Outreach Enabled
When ON — the engine generates an AI-suggested reply for each matched lead and sends SMS/email alerts for HOT and WARM signals.
When OFF — leads are still captured and scored, but no messages are sent and no reply is generated.

Alert Mode
Set ALERT_MODE=owner in .env to send all alerts to you.
Set ALERT_MODE=client to send directly to each client's email/phone.
